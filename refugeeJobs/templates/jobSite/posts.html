General CV Writing Tips

  • Keep it Concise: Limit your CV to two pages. Focus on relevant experiences and skills.
  • Tailor Your CV: Customize your CV for each job application. Highlight the skills and experiences that match the job requirements.
  • Use Action Verbs: Start bullet points with action verbs like managed, developed, coordinated, etc., to demonstrate your achievements.
  • Include Achievements: Where possible, include measurable achievements to showcase your contributions.
  • Professional Layout: Use a clean and professional layout with clear headings and bullet points for easy readability.
  • Proofread: Always proofread your CV for errors in spelling and grammar. Consider asking someone else to review it as well.
